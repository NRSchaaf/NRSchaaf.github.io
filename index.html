Hi. I'm Nathan Schaaf.

I'm a seasoned professional with a passion for leveraging data-driven insights to drive strategic decision-making in business settings. I am pursuing a Master's of Science in Data Science and Business Analytics, and I hold an Associate and Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with a Concentration in Data Science.

I've spent over 18 years at Norfolk Southern Corporation, a Fortune 300 railway company, where I've specialized in Agile methodologies, data analysis and have used machine learning to make impactful decisions. My journey into data science began there, and now, as I pursue my Master's degree, I'm looking for new challenges and opportunities to deepen my knowledge and skills in advanced statistical methods, machine learning and AI techniques, and business acumen.

My goal is to leverage this comprehensive education and hands-on experience to make meaningful contributions in industry as a data scientist, driving strategic decision-making, optimizing business processes, and unlocking new opportunities for growth and enhancement.

My Experience

Here are some of my more recent and meaningful experiences.

Manager Safety & Environmental Systems & Projects

As Product Owner of the Incident Management System, I was responsible for facilitating the collection of business needs, understanding those needs, and translating those needs into solutions. I also was responsible for using data and data science in interdisciplinary ways to generate new knowledge, understanding, and advancement in relationsihps with our business partherns. I used agile methodologies, machine learning techniques, visual analytics, and creative problem solving to meet regulatory requirements, improve business processes, and control costs.

Head of East Palestine Data Portal Taskforce

On February 3, 2023 at 8:55pm EST a Norfolk Southern freight train carrying hazardous materials derailed near the city of East Palestine, OH. On February 5th, I was nominated as Head of the East Palestine Data Portal and tasked with quickly establishing a robust and scalable solution to share data between Norfolk Southern, Incident Command, various contractors and consultants, lawfirms, and various federal, state, and local agencies. The solution created allowed for the collaboration of over 18 organizations and over 200 people.

Web Development Lead for E2M

Equity in Memory and Memorial (E2M) is a peacebuilding initiative that provides substantive steps towards creating equity in memory and memorials of enslaved African American burial grounds in the city of Charlotte and beyond. Led by Dr. Julia Robinson Moore, E2M investigates the complexities of Black and White race relations through the context of the Presbyterian Church. I am leading a team to build a website where members of the community can explore their heritage, virtually visit, engage and collaborate with the project, and to create a digital memorialization.

I can proudly say that I am making a positive impact at the organizations and within in the communities I serve.
