Head of East Palestine Data Portal Taskforce

On February 3, 2023 at 8:55pm EST a Norfolk Southern freight train carrying hazardous materials derailed near the city of East Palestine, OH. On February 5th, I was nominated as Head of the East Palestine Data Portal Task Force and charged to quickly establish a robust and scalable solution to share data between Norfolk Southern, Incident Command, various contractors and consultants, lawfirms, and various federal, state, and local agencies. The solution created allowed for the collaboration of over 18 organizations and over 200 people.
